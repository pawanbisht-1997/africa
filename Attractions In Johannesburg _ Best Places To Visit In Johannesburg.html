Johannesburg Travel Guide

Johannesburg city of South Africa is a hub for culture, food and art. With its art galleries, museum, food trails and vibrant cafes, Johannesburg is evolving as a diverse city that caters every need of tourist. One can even discover a vast option for accommodation from the budget stay to backer hostels and a luxurious hotel stay during the Johannesburg city tour. The developing lifestyle is rapidly boosting up the essence of Johannesburg. The city of Johannesburg, known to be the home of Nelson Mandela has some of the best museums and historical site preserving the history of South Africa. Today the development has enhanced the tourism in Johannesburg with some interesting restaurants, cafes, galleries and art studios. While travelling around the city, you can explore the wildlife at a favourite lion park, discover the fantastic tourist attraction at Johannesburg, enjoy a walk along the vibrant trails of gardens, and plan a day tour to the nearby spots in Pretoria. The tour to Johannesburg city of South Africa will offer you the entire list of the fantastic places that one can explore during the visit. Concluding the features of the city, your Johannesburg tour package will include everything from the sightseeing, eateries, accommodation and the vibrant nightlife.

Places To visit In Johannesburg

Gold Reef City

With the amusement parks, historical galleries and shaft of the Crown Mines, Gold Reef city hides a lot for the tourist to discover. The park is well designed with exhilarating theme-park rides and petting zoo that makes it an ideal spot for Johannesburg day tour.

Soweto

The Soweto takes you to the historical tour of Johannesburg that depicts the era when the black laborers used to stay away from the central city at corrugated houses. The Hector Pieterson, Memorial and Museum at Soweto, was built to honor the students who protested for the rights and Soweto development.

Constitution Hill

Constitution Hill is one of the famous attractions at Johannesburg that you cannot miss during your tour. This historical building is divided into four sections, as the Old Fort, the Number Four Jail, the Women's Jail and the Awaiting Trial Block.

Holy Hill

Holy Hills in Johannesburg South Africa is one of the isolated yet a beautiful place to visit. Lying at the center of the city, the Holy hill is the best tourist place at Johannesburg, ideal for a half day tour, or a leisure visit.

Maboneng Precinct

Lying at the centre of the town, Maboneng Precinct is a famous spot at Johannesburg that offers a fusion of art and culture. Discover the charming cafes, eateries, museums and shopping complex to add more fun to your tour.

Apartheid Museum

Discover the rise and the fall, the segregation and cruelty faced by that era of South Africa at Apartheid Museum. The illustrated architectural and sculptural demonstration of the apartheid system narrates the entire history of South Africa.

Cradle of Humankind

The UNESCO World Heritage Site at Johannesburg city attracts the tourist for its primitive remaining of human civilization and the diverse wildlife. This site is known as the palaeontological zones that will let you explore the roots of mankind and the Sterkfontein Caves.

Lion Park

The Lion Park is one of the famous tourist attractions in Johannesburg city, that houses the population of majestic Asian lion along with the community of cheetah and wild dog. The park opens up activities like the guided game drives animal interactions and self-drives.

Hector Pieterson Museum

Discover the entire series of events and circumstance that occurred during the anti-Apartheid Soweto Uprising at Hector Pieterson Museum. This attraction of Johannesburg was built in memory of 12-year-old who was shot dead during the Soweto moment.

Nelson Mandela National Museum

The Mandela House in Soweto is the most visited attraction during the Johannesburg tour that is now converted into a museum as a memorial. The historical site offers a tour of the entire life journey of Nelson Mandela.

Best Time To Visit Johannesburg

The best time to visit Johannesburg is from September to April when the summers have ended and winters are about to set in. This time, this magnificent city of Johannesburg experiences a fairly moderate climate. The morning are pleasant followed by sunny afternoons which makes it best time to visit Johannesburg.  The other months are not bad as well as the city has dry and mild weather all around the year. Explore the famous attractions around with comfort and witness the best view of the surrounding plateaus, gushing streams and the starry nights.  The months from March to May are considered to be the ideal to visit Johannesburg. The moderate temperature with affordable rates makes it the perfect season for Johannesburg tour. 

Things To Do In Johannesburg

Let the experts guide you to the best of this mesmerizing destination

Day tour to Pretoria

Pretoria is one of best attraction near Johannesburg for a day tour to discover the stunning architectural buildings, monuments, and museums at Pretoria. This beautiful city with the purple ting of purple jacarandas houses the abundance of flora and fauna.

Game drive at Pilanesberg National Park

Pilanesberg National Park houses some of the magnificent wildlife as 300 spices of birds and is known for its rich ecosystem. This is one of the famous game reserves in Johannesburg city where you can explore Africa's Big Five.

Johannesburg city and cultural tour

Pick some of the best museums of South Africa at Johannesburg that are known for their ancient records and magnificent artwork. Exploring the history and the ancient culture of South Africa is the best tour in Johannesburg, you must not miss.

Climbing up the Carlton Centre

Rising up high for the incredible view of the city can be the best experience during the Johannesburg tour. The Carlton Centre is the 50 story building in downtown Johannesburg, with a height of 223 meters.

Full day Soweto visit

The Soweto city will engage your entire day for the best day tour in Johannesburg. The city exhibits the best museums like the Apartheid Museum, preserving the facts and significance of that South African era.

Spending a day with the Lions

The Lion Park housing the endemic Asian Lion offers you the adventure and thrill to experience at Johannesburg city. Enjoy the safari spotting the Big Five and a special interaction tour where you can learn about their habitat.

The entertainment at Gold Reef

The Gold reef tour at Johannesburg is one of the best historical site that even houses the best theme park for a complete day tour. So if the history doesn’t fascinate you, you can surely enjoy the amusing rides there.

The tour of Lesedi Cultural village

With the different tribes and culture of South Africa, the Lesedi Cultural Village offers a complete day tour package of Johannesburg. The cultural village exhibits the art, lifestyle and culture of Zulu, Pedi, Xhosa, Basotho, Nguni and Ndebele tribes.

Visiting the Monte casino

A hub for entertainment and leisure, Monte Casino is a complex with the best hotels, two theaters, a cinema and dozens of restaurants. The area bears a resemblance to the ancient Tuscan village, attracting the tourist with its lively vibes.

Exploring the city life

Spend a day at Johannesburg exploring the local attractions and the best complex to eat and shop. The Baha Taco, District six eatery, the potato shed and town are some of the budget and handpicked cafes one must try during the Johannesburg city tour.

How to Reach Johannesburg

Reaching Johannesburg is quite convenient due to the network of roads and flights. One can enjoy the city tour of Johannesburg via cabs, private vehicles, buses and trains.

Via air- The international and domestic terminals at Johannesburg city are connected to the major countries of the world along with the cities in and around South Africa.

The land route- Buses and trains are generally used by the locals to commute in and around the city of Johannesburg. The trains and roads are connecting Johannesburg with Pretoria, Durban, Zimbabwe and Zambia.
